Mega Space War

Summary

This game starts out with a few simple rules but the rules can change along with gameplay.

Cool Factors

  • Hundreds of different combinations.
  • You never know who could win until the very end.
  • Poker sized, UV coated, linen-textured cards.
  • Fun for the whole family!

Details

  • 2-4 Players
  • Less than 30 minutes typical gameplay
  • 8 years or older suggested
To start playing: Shuffle the cards. The youngest player goes first. On each turn draw one card and you may play one card. Unless rules specify otherwise, only one goal can be active at any time and only one rule can be active at any time. You should try to keep your cards secret from other players.
The game starts out without any goal, but players can change the goal (the goal decides who wins the game). There are two cards that if a player can get them, they win automatically (Warp Drive). There is also an Alien card that makes you lose (You can take these cards out if you prefer). Some example Goals are "Have the most cards" and "Have the least cards". With all of these dynamic rules, gameplay can be very unpredictable and fun! The main goal is to have fun!
