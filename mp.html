Magic Pyramid

About

Two games in one with multiple ways to customize! Play cooperative or competitive mode in easy, moderate, or hard varieties.

Play as one of four characters: the Camel, the Black Cat, the Ghost Queen, or Ra.

Encounter traps set up by the ancient egyptians on your way to the top of the pyramid. You also might find items that help you in your journey, like potions or magic scrolls.

Basic Rules

Each character has different strengths and weaknesses. Players work together to reach the end. Each player has health points that they might lose if they cannot avoid a trap and other players can help them regain those points.
