Summary

As the name suggests you pretend to mine asteroids in space. You collect rock, water, and exotic matter. Only certain combinations gain you points. Trading with other players adds a lot of fun to this one.

Cool Factors

  • Play as one of several space ships.
  • Uses three colored dice.
  • Fun for the whole family!

Details

  • 2-4 Players
  • Less than 30 minutes typical gameplay
  • 7 years or older suggested
