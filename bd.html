Battle of the Dragons

Under Construction 🚧

Family fun with dragons and castles.

Do you like to have fun? Do you like castles and dragons? This game is for you.

In "Battle of the Dragons", you choose a character so every game is different.

Players can work together to defeat the dragons, but only one player reaches the castle's keep first.

The Basics

To begin, players roll a six-sided die and move that number of spaces. Players might land on a "draw a card" space. If so, pick up the next card from the deck which may be a trap, armor, dragon, or something else.

Each player has health, armor, and sometimes magic points. Each character has a special talent they can use in the battle.

Characters

Players play as one of the following:
  • Knight
  • King
  • Queen
  • Thief
  • Horse
  • Wizard
